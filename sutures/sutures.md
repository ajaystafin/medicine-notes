# SUTURES



## SUTURE REMOVAL
- depends on the tension and location of the suture 
	- face - 3-6 days 
	- neck - 7 days 
	- scalp - 10 days 
	- trunk - 10-14 days 
	- lower limb -14-21 days
- remove too soon - no strength 
- remove too late - infection, scarring(so remove early in face), tissue reaction
